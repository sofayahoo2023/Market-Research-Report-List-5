<p><p>Disposable bioreactors are used in the biopharmaceutical industry for cell culture applications. Wave-induced Motion SUBs utilize a rocking motion to provide mixing and aeration in the bioreactor. Stirred SUBs use mechanical agitation to mix the culture and provide oxygenation. Other types of disposable bioreactors may include vertical-wheel SUBs or air-lift SUBs. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications in the biopharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
