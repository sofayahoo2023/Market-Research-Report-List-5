<p><strong>The GaN FET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Depletion Mode</li><li>Enhancement Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GaN FETs are categorized into two main types: Depletion mode and Enhancement mode. Depletion mode GaN FETs are normally on and require a negative voltage to turn off, making them suitable for applications where reliability is crucial. Enhancement mode GaN FETs are normally off and require a positive voltage to turn on, offering lower power consumption and higher efficiency. Both types have their unique advantages and are used in various markets such as power supplies, RF amplifiers, and motor control systems.</p></p>
