<p><ul><li>“Up Running” Hammer Mill</li><li>“Down Running” Hammer Mill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hammermills Market consists of two main types – "Up Running" Hammer Mill and "Down Running" Hammer Mill. The "Up Running" Hammer Mill operates with the rotor spinning in the same direction as the material being fed into the mill, resulting in a higher impact and size reduction. On the other hand, the "Down Running" Hammer Mill, has a rotor spinning in the opposite direction, creating a different cutting and shearing action. Both types cater to different market needs and applications within the industry.</p></p>
