Carbon steel pipes are made from iron and carbon, providing high strength and durability. Stainless steel pipes contain chromium and nickel, offering resistance to corrosion and high temperatures. Alloy steel pipes are made from a combination of different metals, providing specific properties such as increased tensile strength or resistance to particular chemicals.</p></p>
