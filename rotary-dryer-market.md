<p><p>Rotary dryers can be broadly categorized into direct-heated rotary dryers and indirect rotary dryers based on their heating methods. Direct-heated rotary dryers utilize direct contact between the material and hot gases or air to dry the material. Indirect rotary dryers, on the other hand, use an external source of heat to dry the material through a metal wall. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries such as mining, agriculture, and manufacturing.</p></p>
