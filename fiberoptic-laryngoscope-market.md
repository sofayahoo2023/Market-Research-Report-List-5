<p><p>Fiberoptic Laryngoscope market consists of two main types: Rigid Fiberoptic Laryngoscopy and Flexible Fiberoptic Laryngoscope. Flexible Fiberoptic Laryngoscope, on the other hand, utilizes a flexible scope with fiberoptic technology to access hard-to-reach areas of the larynx. Both types offer improved visibility and precision during laryngeal examinations and surgeries, catering to different clinical needs.</p></p>
