<p><p>The Relapsing Multiple Sclerosis treatment market can be classified into different types such as Immunomodulatory, Immunosuppressive, Interferons, and others. Immunomodulatory drugs help regulate the immune system to reduce inflammation in the central nervous system. Immunosuppressive medications work by suppressing the immune response to prevent attacks on the nervous system. Interferons are proteins that help regulate the immune response to reduce inflammation and slow the progression of MS. Other treatments may include corticosteroids, plasma exchanges, or stem cell transplants.</p></p>
