<p><p>Inhalation Therapy Nebulizer market is segmented into three main types: Pneumatic Nebulizers, Ultrasonic Nebulizers, and Mesh Nebulizers. Pneumatic Nebulizers use compressed air to convert liquid medication into a mist for inhalation. Ultrasonic Nebulizers utilize ultrasonic waves to produce a fine mist. Mesh Nebulizers use a vibrating mesh to generate aerosol particles. Each type offers specific advantages such as portability, efficiency, and ease of use, catering to different patient needs and preferences in the inhalation therapy market.</p></p>
