<p><p>Multi-layering chip inductors are categorized into two main types based on the core material used: ceramic core and magnetic core. Ceramic core multi-layering chip inductors are made from layers of ceramic material, providing high stability and low loss at high frequencies. On the other hand, magnetic core multi-layering chip inductors use a magnetic core material, offering high magnetic permeability for improved inductance values. Both types cater to different market needs and applications, providing a range of options for designers and manufacturers in the electronics industry.</p></p>
