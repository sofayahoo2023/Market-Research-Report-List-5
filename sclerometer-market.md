<p><p>Sclerometer market types include Brinell, Vickers, Rockwell, and others. Brinell Sclerometer measures hardness using a large force and a hard ball indenter. Vickers Sclerometer uses a small pyramid-shaped diamond indenter to assess hardness. Rockwell Sclerometer measures hardness by applying a preliminary force before using a harder ball or cone-shaped indenter. Other types of Sclerometers may use different methods and indenter shapes to determine hardness in various materials, providing a range of options for different applications.</p></p>
