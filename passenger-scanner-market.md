<p><p>Passenger scanners are security devices used in airports and other transportation hubs to screen passengers for potential threats. Two common types of passenger scanners are X-ray scanners and millimeter wave scanners. X-ray passenger scanners use high-energy radiation to create images of the inside of a person's luggage and clothing, while millimeter wave passenger scanners use electromagnetic waves to detect hidden objects on a person's body. Both types of scanners are vital tools in ensuring the safety and security of travelers.</p></p>
