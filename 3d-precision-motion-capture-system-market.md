<p><p>3D Precision Motion Capture System market consists of Optical System, Inertial System, and Other types. Optical systems use cameras to track reflective markers on a subject, providing highly accurate data but requiring line-of-sight. Inertial systems utilize sensors to measure motion directly on the body, allowing for more freedom of movement but with slightly lower precision. Other systems include electromagnetic and markerless technologies, each offering unique advantages in terms of accuracy, ease of use, and cost.</p></p>
