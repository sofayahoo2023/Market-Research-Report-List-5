<p><p>Rotary positive blowers are classified into different types based on the design of their lobes. Straight Bi-lobe blowers have two lobes, Straight Tri-lobe blowers have three lobes, and Twisted Tri-lobe blowers have twisted lobes for increased efficiency. Helical Screw blowers operate with a pair of helical rotors. Other types of rotary positive blowers include regenerative blowers and claw type blowers. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, durability, and performance in various applications.</p></p>
