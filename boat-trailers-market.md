<p><p>Bunk and roller boat trailers are two common types available in the market. Roller trailers, on the other hand, have sets of rollers that allow the boat to be easily launched and loaded. These trailers are better suited for smaller boats and for users looking for convenience and ease of use. Both types come in a variety of sizes and configurations to accommodate different boat sizes and weights.</p></p>
