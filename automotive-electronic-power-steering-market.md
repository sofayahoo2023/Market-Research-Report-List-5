<p><strong>The Automotive Electronic Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Electronic Power Steering (EPS) market encompasses various types, specifically Column-EPS (C-EPS), Pinion-EPS (P-EPS), and Rack-EPS (R-EPS). C-EPS utilizes a torque sensor on the steering column, while P-EPS incorporates the sensor into the steering gear. R-EPS, on the other hand, integrates the sensor directly into the rack mechanism. Each type has its own set of advantages and drawbacks, catering to different preferences and requirements of automakers and consumers in terms of performance, cost, and space utilization.</p></p>
