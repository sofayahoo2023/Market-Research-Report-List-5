<p><p>Scrubber systems are devices used to reduce pollution from exhaust gases in industries and ships. The market offers various types of scrubbers including Open Loop, Closed Loop, Hybrid, Dry, and Membrane scrubbers. Open Loop scrubbers use seawater for scrubbing emissions, while Closed Loop scrubbers use fresh water with alkaline solutions. Hybrid scrubbers combine both open and closed loop technologies. Dry scrubbers rely on a dry sorbent to remove pollutants, while membrane scrubbers use membrane filtration for cleaning emissions. Each type offers unique benefits for emissions control.</p></p>
