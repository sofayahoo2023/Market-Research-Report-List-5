<p><p>Antenna Measurement System Market includes near-field and far-field systems. Near-field systems involve placing the antenna close to the measurement probe, allowing for precise analysis of antenna performance. Far-field systems, on the other hand, measure the antenna's radiation pattern at a distance, providing a broader view of its performance in real-world conditions. Both types of systems play a crucial role in optimizing antenna design and ensuring efficient wireless communication systems.</p></p>
