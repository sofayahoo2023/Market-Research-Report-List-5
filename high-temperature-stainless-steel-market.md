These grades, such as type 310 and 316, are commonly used in applications requiring elevated temperatures. On the other hand, High Temperature Ferritic Grades, like type 441 and 446, are known for their high-temperature oxidation resistance and thermal stability. These grades are often utilized in applications where both elevated temperatures and corrosion resistance are crucial.</p></p>
