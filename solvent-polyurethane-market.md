<p><p>Solvent polyurethane is categorized into three main types: polyether, polyester, and mixed type. Polyether type is known for its high flexibility and resistance to water, making it ideal for outdoor applications. Polyester type offers high strength and durability, making it suitable for industrial purposes. Mixed type combines the properties of both polyether and polyester, providing a balanced combination of flexibility and strength. Each type has its own unique characteristics and advantages, catering to different needs in various industries.</p></p>
