<p><p>Current sensing transformers are devices used to measure electrical current in a system. There are three main types of current sensing transformers: wound, toroidal, and bar. Wound transformers are made by winding wire around a magnetic core, providing accurate current measurement. Toroidal transformers have a doughnut-shaped core which reduces electromagnetic interference. Bar transformers have a straight core and are commonly used in high-power applications. Each type of transformer has unique characteristics that make it suitable for different applications based on factors such as accuracy, size, and cost.</p></p>
