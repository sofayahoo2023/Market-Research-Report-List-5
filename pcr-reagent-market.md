<p><p>PCR reagents are essential components used in polymerase chain reaction (PCR) for amplifying DNA. The market for PCR reagents can be segmented into primer, enzyme, DNTP, template, and buffer solution segments. Primers are short nucleotide sequences that initiate DNA synthesis, enzymes like Taq polymerase amplify DNA, DNTPs provide nucleotides for building DNA strands, templates are the DNA samples to be replicated, and buffer solutions maintain the optimal pH and salt concentration for PCR reactions. Each type of reagent plays a crucial role in PCR amplification.</p></p>
