<p><p>Floor springs are divided into several market types based on their stopping angles. The 90 degrees stop floor spring stops the door at a 90-degree angle when opened, providing a traditional opening range. The 105 degrees stop floor spring allows the door to swing open a bit further, providing a wider opening range. Non-stop floor springs allow the door to swing open a full 180 degrees without stopping at a specific angle. Other variations of floor springs may offer different stopping angles or additional features to suit specific door and building requirements.</p></p>
