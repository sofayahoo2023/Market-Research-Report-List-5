<p><p>Workholding devices are essential tools for securing workpieces during machining processes. In the milling/drilling workholding market, devices like vises and clamps are commonly used to hold materials in place for precision cutting. Turning workholding devices, such as chucks and collets, are used for securing rotating workpieces during lathing operations. Grinding workholding devices, like magnetic chucks and fixtures, are utilized for holding materials in place during grinding processes. In the EDM workholding market, electrostatic chucks and collet systems are commonly used to grip workpieces for electrical discharge machining operations.</p></p>
