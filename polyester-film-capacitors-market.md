<p><strong>The Polyester Film Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plain Polyester Film Capacitors</li><li>Metallised Polyester Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester film capacitors are categorized into two main types: Plain Polyester Film Capacitors and Metallised Polyester Film Capacitors. Plain polyester film capacitors utilize a plain polyester film as the dielectric material, while metallised polyester film capacitors have a thin metal layer deposited on the polyester film to increase their capacitance. Both types of capacitors are widely used in various electronic applications due to their excellent insulation properties, high dielectric strength, and reliability.</p></p>
