<p><strong>The Coronary Guidewires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steel-based</li><li>Nitinol-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coronary guidewires are classified into two main types: steel-based and nitinol-based. Steel-based guidewires are made of stainless steel, offering high strength and torque control. They are commonly used for straightforward procedures. On the other hand, nitinol-based guidewires are made of a nickel-titanium alloy, providing flexibility and shape memory. They are preferred for complex interventions and navigating tortuous vessels. Both types of guidewires play a crucial role in guiding interventional procedures in coronary arteries, with each offering unique advantages based on the procedure requirements.</p></p>
