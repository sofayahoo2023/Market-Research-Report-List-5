<p><p>Waterjet robots market consists of various types of jets including continuous jet, pulse jet, and cavitation jet. Continuous jet systems provide a steady stream of water for consistent cutting. Pulse jet systems use intermittent bursts of water for more precise cutting. Cavitation jet systems create bubbles in the water stream to enhance cutting efficiency. Each type of jet offers unique advantages and applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing. Manufacturers can choose the type of jet that best suits their cutting needs.</p></p>
