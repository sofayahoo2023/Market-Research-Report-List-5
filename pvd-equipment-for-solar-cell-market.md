<p><p>PVD Equipment for Solar Cell Market includes three main types: Thermal Evaporation, Sputter Deposition, and Arc Vapor Deposition. </p><p>Thermal Evaporation involves heating a material until it evaporates and deposits onto a substrate. Sputter Deposition uses plasma to dislodge atoms from a target material, which then deposit onto a substrate. Arc Vapor Deposition uses an electric arc to vaporize a target material, which is then deposited onto a substrate.</p><p>Each method offers unique advantages and is used in the production of solar cells.</p></p>
