<p><p>Surface Mounted Technology (SMT) is a method used in the electronic industry to mount electronic components onto printed circuit boards. It involves soldering the components directly onto the surface of the circuit board, rather than passing their leads through holes in the board. This results in a more efficient and cost-effective manufacturing process, as well as a higher component density on the board.</p><p>The Surface Mounted Technology Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for compact and portable electronic devices, as well as the shift towards automation and industrial IoT. Additionally, the adoption of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning in manufacturing processes is driving the growth of the SMT market.</p><p>Moreover, the rise in demand for consumer electronics, automotive electronics, and medical devices is further boosting the market growth.
