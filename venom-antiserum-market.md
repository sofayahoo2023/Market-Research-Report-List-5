<p><p>Venom antiserum is available in two main types: polyvalent and monovalent. Polyvalent antiserum is effective against multiple types of venom from different species, making it broad-spectrum. On the other hand, monovalent antiserum is specific to one type of venom, making it more targeted and suitable for cases where the venom source is known. The choice between polyvalent and monovalent antiserum depends on the specific venom exposure and the desired level of specificity in treatment.</p></p>
