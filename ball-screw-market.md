<p><ul><li>InternalCirculationBallscrew</li><li>ExternalCirculationBallscrew</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball screw market can be classified into two types based on the circulation of balls within the system - Internal Circulation Ballscrew and External Circulation Ballscrew. In Internal Circulation Ballscrew, the balls circulate inside the nut and do not come out, providing better protection and sealing. On the other hand, External Circulation Ballscrew has the balls circulating outside the nut, allowing for higher load capacity and longer life. Both types cater to different industrial needs and applications, offering unique advantages and functionalities.</p></p>
