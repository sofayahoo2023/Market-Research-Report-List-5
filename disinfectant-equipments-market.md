<p><p>Disinfectant equipments are used in various applications for physical sterilization, radiation sterilization, and chemical sterilization. Physical sterilization involves processes like heat, steam, and filtration to kill microorganisms. Radiation sterilization uses methods like UV light or gamma radiation to disinfect surfaces or medical equipment. Chemical sterilization involves the use of disinfectants like bleach or hydrogen peroxide to kill bacteria and viruses. These three methods are essential for maintaining a clean and sterile environment in industries such as healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
