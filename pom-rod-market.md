<p><p>POM rods are available in two main types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene rods are made from a single type of material, resulting in a strong and rigid product. On the other hand, co-polyoxymethylene rods are made from a blend of different materials, offering a balance between strength and flexibility. Both types of POM rods are widely used in various industries due to their excellent mechanical properties, chemical resistance, and low friction coefficient.</p></p>
