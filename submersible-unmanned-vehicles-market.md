<p><p>Submersible Unmanned Vehicles (SUVs) consist of Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs). ROVs are controlled by an operator from the surface and are commonly used for tasks such as underwater exploration, maintenance, and salvage operations. AUVs, on the other hand, operate independently without human intervention and are used for tasks like ocean mapping, scientific research, and environmental monitoring. Both types of SUVs are crucial tools for underwater activities in various industries including oil and gas, marine research, and defense.</p></p>
