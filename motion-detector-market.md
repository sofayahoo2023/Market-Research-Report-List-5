<p><p>Motion detector market can be broadly categorized into three types: accelerometer, gyroscope, and magnetometer. Accelerometers are used to measure acceleration, gyroscope measures angular velocity, and magnetometers detects magnetic fields. Combo sensor market combines these three types of sensors into one device for more accurate and comprehensive motion detection. These sensors are widely used in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, healthcare, and robotics for applications like motion tracking, navigation, and gesture recognition.</p></p>
