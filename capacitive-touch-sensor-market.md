<p><strong>The Capacitive Touch Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Capacitive Sensing</li><li>Projected Capacitive Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive touch sensors are available in two main types: Surface Capacitive Sensing and Projected Capacitive Sensing. Surface Capacitive Sensing technology uses a single electrode layer to detect touch on the surface of the screen, making it cost-effective and suitable for applications like self-service kiosks. In contrast, Projected Capacitive Sensing uses multiple layers of electrodes to create a more accurate, responsive touch interface commonly found in smartphones and tablets. Both technologies cater to different market needs based on sensitivity, cost, and application requirements.</p></p>
