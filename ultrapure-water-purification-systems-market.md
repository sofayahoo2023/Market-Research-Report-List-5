<p><p>Ultrapure water purification systems market consists of three main types: Reverse Osmosis, Electrodeionization (EDI), and Ion Exchange. Reverse Osmosis systems use a membrane to remove impurities from water, while EDI systems use electricity to remove ions. Ion Exchange systems use resin beads to exchange ions in water. These systems are vital in industries such as pharmaceuticals, electronics, and power generation where highly purified water is necessary. Each type of system offers unique benefits and is used based on specific requirements and end-uses.</p></p>
