<p><p>Electrical safety barriers are devices used to prevent electrical shocks and fires by isolating electrical circuits and equipment. The market for these barriers can be segmented into three types: 1-channel, 2-channel, and multi-channel. 1-channel barriers are designed to isolate one circuit at a time, while 2-channel barriers offer dual isolation for added protection. Multi-channel barriers can isolate multiple circuits simultaneously, making them ideal for complex electrical systems. Each type offers varying levels of safety and convenience based on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
