<p><p>Water filter housing market consists of various types like Reverse Osmosis, Ozone Water Purifier, Activated Carbon, and Distiller. Reverse Osmosis filters water through a semipermeable membrane, removing impurities. Ozone water purifiers use ozone gas to disinfect and purify water. Activated carbon filters trap and remove contaminants from water. Distillers boil water, collecting the steam and leaving impurities behind. Each type of water filter housing caters to different needs and preferences, providing safe and clean drinking water for consumers.</p></p>
