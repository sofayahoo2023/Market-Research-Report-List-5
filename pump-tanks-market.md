<p><p>Pump tanks come in two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical pump tanks are designed to stand upright, taking up less space and are commonly used for residential and small commercial applications. Horizontal pump tanks, on the other hand, are laid on their side and are usually larger in size. They are typically used in larger commercial and industrial settings where higher water storage capacity is needed. Both types of pump tanks serve the same function of storing and pressurizing water for various applications.</p></p>
