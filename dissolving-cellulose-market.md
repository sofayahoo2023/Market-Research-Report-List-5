<p><p>Dissolving cellulose market types include alkali wood pulp or sulfate wood pulp, and wood pulp of sulfite. Alkali wood pulp, also known as sulfate wood pulp, is produced through a chemical process using sodium hydroxide and sodium sulfide. This type of pulp is commonly used in the production of viscose fibers. On the other hand, wood pulp of sulfite is produced through a different chemical process using sulfurous acid. It is also used in the production of dissolving cellulose products.</p></p>
