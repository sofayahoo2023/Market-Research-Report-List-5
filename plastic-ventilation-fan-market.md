<p><p>Plastic ventilation fans come in two main types: Axial and Centrifugal. Axial fans are designed to move air parallel to the shaft, making them ideal for applications requiring high air flow at low pressure. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, are designed to move air at right angles to the intake of the fan, creating a higher pressure and more efficient air movement. Both types of fans are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial ventilation systems for controlling indoor air quality and temperature.</p></p>
