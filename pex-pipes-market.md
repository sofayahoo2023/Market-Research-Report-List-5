<p><p>PEX pipes, also known as cross-linked polyethylene pipes, come in three main types - PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa pipes are manufactured using the Engle method, resulting in better flexibility and high-temperature resistance. PE-Xb pipes, produced using the Silane method, offer good chemical resistance and low-temperature flexibility. PE-Xc pipes, made using the Peroxide method, provide a balance between flexibility and strength. Each type caters to different needs in industries such as plumbing, heating, and cooling systems.</p></p>
