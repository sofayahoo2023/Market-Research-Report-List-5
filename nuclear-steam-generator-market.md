<p><p>Nuclear steam generators are categorized into two main types based on their orientation: Horizontal Steam Generators and Vertical Steam Generators. Horizontal Steam Generators are larger in size and are typically used in pressurized water reactors. They require less space and maintenance compared to vertical steam generators. Vertical Steam Generators, on the other hand, are smaller in size and are commonly used in boiling water reactors. They are known for their efficiency and compact design, making them ideal for smaller nuclear power plants.</p></p>
