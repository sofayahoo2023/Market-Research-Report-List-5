<p><p>Linear actuators are devices used to convert energy into motion in a straight line. There are three main types of linear actuators: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to create movement, electric actuators use electric motors, and hydraulic actuators use fluid pressure. Each type has its own advantages and applications, with pneumatic actuators being suited for quick and precise movements, electric actuators for quiet and efficient operation, and hydraulic actuators for heavy-duty tasks.</p></p>
