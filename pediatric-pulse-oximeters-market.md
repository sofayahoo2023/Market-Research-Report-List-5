<p><p>Pediatric pulse oximeters are medical devices used to measure oxygen levels in children. There are two main types of sensors used in pediatric pulse oximeters: wrap sensors and clip sensors. Wrap sensors are designed to be wrapped around a child's finger, toe, or foot, providing a secure and comfortable fit. Clip sensors, on the other hand, can be clipped onto the child's finger or earlobe. Both types of sensors are essential in accurately monitoring oxygen levels in pediatric patients and are commonly used in hospitals and clinical settings.</p></p>
