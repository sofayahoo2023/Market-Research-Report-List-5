<p><p>Vehicle LiDAR market is segmented into three main types: Mechanical LiDAR, Solid State LiDAR, and Hybrid Solid-state LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR systems use rotating mirrors to scan their surroundings, while Solid State LiDAR uses no moving parts, resulting in a more compact and reliable system. Hybrid Solid-state LiDAR combines the best features of both types, offering a balance between performance and cost. Each type has its strengths and weaknesses, catering to different preferences and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
