<p><p>Thermopile modules are often categorized into three main types: array modules, multichannel modules, and other variations. Array modules consist of multiple thermopile elements arranged in a matrix pattern, allowing for increased sensitivity and wider detection coverage. Multichannel modules feature multiple thermopile elements in parallel, enabling simultaneous readings from different sources. Other variations of thermopile modules may include custom configurations based on specific application requirements, such as integration with additional sensors or specialized packaging for unique environmental conditions.</p></p>
