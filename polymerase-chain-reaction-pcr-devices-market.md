<p><p>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) devices market is divided into two main types - Digital PCR and Real-time PCR. Digital PCR allows for absolute quantification of nucleic acids by partitioning a sample into thousands of individual reactions. Real-time PCR, on the other hand, enables continuous monitoring of the amplification process in real-time, providing quantitative data on the amount of DNA present in a sample. Both types of PCR devices play a crucial role in various applications such as genetic testing, disease diagnosis, and research.</p></p>
