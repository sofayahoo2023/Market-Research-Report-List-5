<p><p>Metallurgical coal market is divided into various types based on their quality and characteristics. Hard Coking Coals (HCC) are high-quality coals used in steel production. Medium Coking Coal falls between HCC and Semi-soft Coking Coal (SSCC) in terms of quality. SSCC is lower in quality than HCC but still suitable for steel manufacturing. Pulverized Coal Injection (PCI) Coal is a lower-quality coal used in steel mills for injection into blast furnaces to reduce overall costs. Each type serves a specific purpose in the steel-making process.</p></p>
