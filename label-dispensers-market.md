<p><p>Label dispensers are categorized into two main types: Manual label dispensers and Automatic label dispensers. Manual label dispensers require operators to manually peel off and apply labels to products, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations with lower label application volumes. On the other hand, Automatic label dispensers feature motorized systems that automatically dispense labels, making them ideal for high-volume labeling processes. Automatic label dispensers are more efficient and quicker than manual label dispensers, increasing productivity and reducing labor costs for businesses.</p></p>
