<p><p>Variable Data Printing (VDP) refers to the printing of personalized and unique information on each printed piece in a print run. There are two main types of VDP markets: Release Liner and Linerless. Release Liner VDP involves printing information on a release liner that can be peeled off and applied to various products. Linerless VDP, on the other hand, eliminates the need for a liner by directly printing the information on the product itself. Both markets cater to different industries and applications based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
