<p><ul><li>Bifenthrins</li><li>Borates</li><li>Sulfuryl Fluorides</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Termite drugs are categorized into different types based on their active ingredients. Bifenthrins are synthetic pyrethroids that are commonly used in termite treatments due to their effectiveness. Borates are natural mineral salts that are non-toxic to humans but lethal to termites. Sulfuryl fluorides are fumigants that penetrate deep into infested wood to eliminate termites. Other market types include chitin synthesis inhibitors and insect growth regulators that disrupt the termites' life cycle. Each type of termite drug offers unique benefits and effectiveness in termite control.</p></p>
