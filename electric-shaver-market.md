<p><ul><li>Foil</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of electric shavers on the market: foil shavers and rotary shavers. Foil shavers have a thin, curved metal foil covering the blades, providing a close shave by lifting the hair before cutting it. On the other hand, rotary shavers have multiple spinning heads that move in a circular motion to capture and cut the hair. The choice between foil and rotary shavers depends on personal preference and skin sensitivity, as both types offer unique benefits in terms of shaving experience and results.</p></p>
