<p><ul><li>UN Type</li><li>UHS Type</li><li>UNS Type</li><li>U Type</li><li>Y Type</li><li>V Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lip seals are commonly categorized into different types based on their designs and applications. UN type seals are versatile and designed for general use. UHS type seals are used for high-speed applications where low friction and heat generation are crucial. UNS type seals are suitable for high-pressure environments. U type seals are designed for heavy-duty applications with minimal leakage. Y type seals are used in applications requiring high sealing performance. V type seals are used for dynamic sealing applications where high speeds and temperatures are present.</p></p>
