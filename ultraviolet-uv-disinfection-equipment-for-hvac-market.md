<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV) disinfection equipment for HVAC systems is available in two main types: air sterilization and coil sterilization. Air sterilization systems use UV lamps to kill airborne pathogens and microorganisms as air passes through the HVAC system, ensuring clean and healthy air circulation. Coil sterilization systems target the coils within the HVAC unit, preventing the growth of mold and bacteria on the surfaces, thus improving energy efficiency and indoor air quality. Both types of UV disinfection equipment play a crucial role in maintaining a safe and hygienic environment.</p></p>
