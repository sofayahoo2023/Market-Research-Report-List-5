<p><p>Wireless power transmission market can be categorized into two main types: Near-Field Technology and Far-Field Technology. Near-Field Technology involves the transmission of power over short distances, typically using magnetic resonance or inductive coupling. This technology is commonly used in applications such as wireless charging pads. Far-Field Technology, on the other hand, enables power transmission over longer distances using technologies like radio waves or lasers. This technology is commonly used for applications requiring power transmission over larger areas, such as wireless power transmission in electric vehicles.</p></p>
