<p><p>Agricultural limestone comes in three main types: Calcitic, Dolomitic, and Other. Calcitic limestone is primarily composed of calcium carbonate, while Dolomitic limestone contains both calcium carbonate and magnesium carbonate. These two types are the most common and widely used in agriculture. Other types of agricultural limestone may include high-magnesium limestone, hydrated lime, and burnt lime. Each type has varying levels of calcium and magnesium, making them suitable for different soil types and pH levels.</p></p>
