<p><p>A Melt Flow Meter is a device used to measure the flow properties of molten plastics. It evaluates the melt flow rate (MFR) or melt volume rate (MVR) of a polymer by extruding a sample through a die of specific dimensions under controlled conditions of temperature and pressure.
