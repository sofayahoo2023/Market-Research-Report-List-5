<p><p>Off-highway tires play a crucial role in various industries including construction, mining, agriculture, and forestry. There are three main types of off-highway tires available in the market: Bias Type, Radial Type, and Solid Type. Bias Type tires are constructed with multiple layers of fabric crisscrossed at a 30-45 degree angle, providing high durability and resistance to punctures. Radial Type tires have fabric layers arranged at 90 degrees to the direction of travel, offering better traction and fuel efficiency. Solid Type tires are made of solid rubber, suitable for heavy-duty applications requiring low maintenance.</p></p>
