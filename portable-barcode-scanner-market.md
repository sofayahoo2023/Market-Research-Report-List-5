<p><strong>The Portable Barcode Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Scanner</li><li>Linear Imager Scanner</li><li>2D Imager Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable barcode scanners come in three main types: laser scanners, linear imager scanners, and 2D imager scanners. Laser scanners use a laser light to read the barcode, making them fast and accurate for scanning traditional linear barcodes. Linear imager scanners use a CCD sensor to capture the barcode image, making them suitable for low-light environments. 2D imager scanners are the most advanced type, capable of reading both traditional linear barcodes and 2D barcodes, making them versatile for various industries.</p></p>
