<p><p>Simultaneous Interpretation Equipment Market includes two main types: Cable and Wireless systems. Cable systems require physical connections between the interpreter's booth and the listener's receivers, providing a stable and secure connection. On the other hand, Wireless systems utilize technology such as infrared or radio waves to transmit the interpreted audio signal without the need for physical cables, offering more flexibility and mobility. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different contexts based on the specific needs of the event or venue.</p></p>
