<p><p>District Heating Pipeline Network Market has two main types of pipelines - Pre-insulated Steel and Polymer pipelines. Pre-insulated Steel pipelines are made of steel pipes with a layer of insulation and a protective outer casing, ensuring durability and efficiency. On the other hand, Polymer pipelines are made of plastic materials like polyethylene or polypropylene, offering flexibility and corrosion resistance. Both types of pipelines are used in district heating systems to transport hot water or steam to multiple buildings in a cost-effective and environmentally friendly manner.</p></p>
