Potassic fertilizers contain potassium for overall plant health and disease resistance. Nitrogenous fertilizers supply nitrogen for leaf growth and overall plant vigor. Other specialty fertilizers may include micronutrients like iron, zinc, and magnesium to address specific nutrient deficiencies in plants. Each type of specialty fertilizer caters to specific plant needs and helps enhance crop yield and quality.</p></p>
