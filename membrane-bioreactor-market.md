<p><p>Membrane bioreactor (MBR) market is segmented into three types: hollow fiber, flat sheet, and multi-tubular. In a hollow fiber system, membranes are arranged in a tube-like structure for efficient filtration. Flat sheet systems use a flat membrane surface for filtration, offering ease of maintenance. Multi-tubular systems feature multiple tubes for filtration, providing high surface area for effective treatment. Each type of MBR offers unique advantages, making them suitable for various applications in wastewater treatment and industrial processes.</p></p>
