<p><p>Asthma devices market includes two main types: Inhalers and Nebulizers. Inhalers are handheld devices that deliver medication directly into the lungs through inhalation, providing quick relief for asthma symptoms. Nebulizers, on the other hand, are machines that convert liquid medication into a fine mist that can be inhaled through a mask or mouthpiece. Nebulizers are often used for individuals who have difficulty using inhalers or require larger doses of medication. Both types of devices are essential in managing asthma effectively.</p></p>
