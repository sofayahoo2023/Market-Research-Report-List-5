<p><p>Vertical Launching System (VLS) is a type of missile launching system used on naval vessels. There are three primary types of VLS markets: Hot Launch, Cold Launch, and Concentric Canister Launch. </p><p>Hot Launch involves igniting the missile inside the canister before it is ejected, allowing for quicker launch times. Cold Launch uses gas pressure to eject the missile from the canister before igniting it, reducing the risk of damage to the ship. Concentric Canister Launch involves a canister with multiple cells that can rotate to a firing position before launch.</p></p>
