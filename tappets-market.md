<p><p>Tappets are components used in engines to control the opening and closing of valves. There are two main types of tappets in the market: flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets have a flat surface that contacts the camshaft, while roller tappets have a rolling surface that reduces friction and wear. Flat tappets are more commonly found in older engines, while roller tappets are preferred in modern high-performance engines due to their improved durability and performance. Both types of tappets play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth operation of an engine.</p></p>
