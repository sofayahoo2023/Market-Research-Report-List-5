<p><p>Influenza antiviral drugs market can be categorized into various types including Oseltamivir, Zanamivir, Peramivir, Adamantanes, and Others. Oseltamivir and Zanamivir are neuraminidase inhibitors commonly used for treating influenza A and B viruses. Peramivir is a newer antiviral drug that can be administered intravenously. Adamantanes are another class of antiviral drugs that target the M2 protein of the influenza virus. The "Others" category includes alternative antiviral drugs used in certain cases of influenza infection.</p></p>
