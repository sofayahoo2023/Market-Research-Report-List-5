<p><p>The Cranial Molding Helmet market consists of two main types: Active Helmets and Passive Helmets. Active Helmets are designed to actively reshape the baby's head by applying pressure to specific areas, usually through adjustable straps or tightening mechanisms. Passive Helmets, on the other hand, work by providing a snug fit to gently guide the baby's head growth. Both types of helmets aim to correct skull deformities in infants and young children, with Active Helmets providing more targeted and adjustable treatment compared to Passive Helmets.</p></p>
