<p><p>Horizontal well drilling markets can be classified into three types based on the radius of the wellbore: long radius horizontal well, medium radius horizontal well, and short radius horizontal well. Long radius horizontal wells have longer wellbores, allowing for greater contact with the reservoir and higher production rates. Medium radius horizontal wells strike a balance between long and short radius wells in terms of reach and productivity. Short radius horizontal wells are typically used in constrained or challenging environments where space is limited.</p></p>
