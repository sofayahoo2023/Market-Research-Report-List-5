<p><strong>The Mechanical Tappets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Tappet</li><li>Roller Tappet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical tappets are used in engines to transfer motion from the camshaft to the valve. There are two main types of mechanical tappets on the market: flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets have a flat contact surface with the camshaft, while roller tappets have a cylindrical shape that rolls along the camshaft surface. Flat tappets are more common in older engines, while roller tappets are preferred in modern engines due to their smoother operation and reduced friction.</p></p>
