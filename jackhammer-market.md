<p><p>Jackhammers are commonly used in construction and demolition work. There are three main types of jackhammers based on their power source: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic jackhammers are powered by compressed air and are lightweight and portable. Electric jackhammers are powered by electricity and are often used in indoor settings due to their low noise levels. Hydraulic jackhammers are powered by hydraulic fluid and are known for their high power and efficiency. Each type of jackhammer has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on the specific needs of the job.</p></p>
