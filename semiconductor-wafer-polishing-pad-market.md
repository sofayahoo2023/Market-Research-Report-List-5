<p><p>Semiconductor wafer polishing pads can be categorized into two main types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are composed of a firmer material, typically polyurethane, that provides more rigidity and durability during the polishing process. On the other hand, soft CMP pads are made of a softer material, like foam, that offers a gentler touch on the delicate semiconductor wafers. Both types of pads are crucial in the semiconductor manufacturing industry for achieving precise and high-quality polishing results.</p></p>
