<p><p>Single zone tunnels in the steam shrink tunnel market feature a single chamber where the steam is evenly distributed to shrink the packaging material. On the other hand, dual zone tunnels have two separate chambers that allow for precise control over the shrinking process by adjusting steam levels in different zones. Single zone tunnels are more common and cost-effective for basic shrink wrapping applications, while dual zone tunnels offer more flexibility and control for specialized packaging requirements.</p></p>
