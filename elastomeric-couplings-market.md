<p><p>Elastomeric couplings come in various types to suit different industrial requirements. Jaw type couplings provide high torque transmission with vibration absorption. Gear type couplings are robust and handle misalignment well. Tire type couplings offer flexibility and damping properties. Dowel pin type couplings are compact and simple to install. Other types include grid, sleeve, and diaphragm couplings, each offering unique benefits such as high misalignment capacity, shock absorption, or precision in torque transmission. Overall, elastomeric couplings cater to diverse applications with their distinct features and advantages.</p></p>
