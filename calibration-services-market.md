<p><p>Calibration services market consists of four main types: Mechanical, Electrical, Thermodynamic, and Others. Mechanical calibration involves adjusting and testing various mechanical instruments and tools, ensuring accurate measurements and functionality. Electrical calibration focuses on calibrating electrical equipment such as meters, analyzers, and generators to maintain precision in electrical measurements. Thermodynamic calibration involves testing and adjusting temperature and pressure measuring devices. The category of "Others" encompasses calibration services for other types of equipment and instruments not covered by the three main categories.</p></p>
