<p><p>Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) certification market consists of three main types: Domain Validated (DV) SSL Certificate, Extended Validation (EV) SSL Certificate, and Organization Validated (OV) SSL Certificate. DV SSL is the most basic, validating only domain ownership. EV SSL provides the highest level of validation, verifying the legal existence and physical location of a website's owner. OV SSL falls in between, verifying the organization's identity. These SSL certificates are crucial for securing online transactions and building trust with website visitors.</p></p>
