GaN transistors offer high power density and frequency capabilities, ideal for small cell and mMIMO applications. GaAs transistors are valued for their high-frequency performance and low noise, often used in millimeter-wave 5G systems. Other types include SiGe and InGaP transistors, each offering unique advantages for specific 5G applications.</p></p>
