<p><strong>The Composite Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Composite Fibres</li><li>Cortical Composite Fibres</li><li>Island Composite Fibres</li><li>Dispersed Primary Fibres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parallel composite fibres are made up of two or more fibres running parallel to each other, providing strength and durability. Cortical composite fibres consist of a central core surrounded by layers of different materials, enhancing flexibility. Island composite fibres have separate islands of fibres dispersed throughout the material, enhancing specific properties in targeted areas. Dispersed primary fibres are evenly distributed throughout the material, providing overall strength and consistency. Each type of composite fibre market offers unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
