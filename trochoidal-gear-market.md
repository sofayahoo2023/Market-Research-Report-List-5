<p><ul><li>Single Stage Cycloidal Gearing</li><li>Double-Stage Cycloidal Gearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single stage cycloidal gearing refers to trochoidal gears with one stage of cycloidal motion, providing precise and smooth gear operation. Double-stage cycloidal gearing involves two stages of cycloidal motion, offering higher gear reduction and torque capacity compared to single stage gearing. Both types are commonly used in industrial machinery, robotics, and other applications requiring high efficiency and accuracy in gear systems. Single stage gearing is suitable for applications with lighter loads, while double-stage gearing is ideal for heavy-duty applications with greater torque requirements.</p></p>
