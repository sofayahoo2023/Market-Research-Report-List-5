<p><strong>The Automatic Fire Sprinkler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Bubbles</li><li>Fusible Alloy</li><li>Stent Type</li><li>Heat Sink Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic fire sprinklers come in various types, including glass bubbles, fusible alloy, stent type, and heat sink type. Glass bubbles are filled with liquid that expands when heated, triggering the sprinklers. Fusible alloy sprinklers have a metal alloy that melts at a specific temperature, releasing water. Stent type sprinklers use a metal stent that collapses when heated, enabling water flow. Heat sink type sprinklers absorb heat and trigger the release of water to suppress fires effectively in different environments.</p></p>
