<p><p>The automotive exterior composites market consists of various types, including compression-molded composites, injection-molded composites, resin-infused composites, and others. Compression-molded composites are formed by compressing resin and fibers in a mold. Injection-molded composites involve injecting resin into a mold under high pressure. Resin-infused composites are created by infusing resin into a preform. The "others" category includes unique composite manufacturing processes used in automotive exterior components. Each type offers specific advantages in terms of cost, strength, and design flexibility.</p></p>
