<p><p>Decoagulant market types include Platelet aggregation inhibitors (PAI), which prevent blood clotting by inhibiting platelet aggregation; Low-molecular-weight Heparin (LMWH), which inhibit certain factors in the blood from clotting; Direct thrombin inhibitors (DTI), which block the action of thrombin in the blood clotting process; Direct factor Xa inhibitors (DFXa), which target factor Xa to prevent blood clotting; Vitamin K antagonists (VKA), which interfere with the body's ability to use vitamin K to form blood clots; and other types of decoagulants that work through different mechanisms to prevent blood clot formation.</p></p>
