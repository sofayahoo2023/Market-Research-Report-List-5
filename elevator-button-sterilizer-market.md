<p><p>The Elevator Button Sterilizer market can be classified into two main types: Internal and External. Internal Elevator Button Sterilizers are installed inside the elevator cabins to automatically sterilize the buttons after every use, ensuring a safe and clean environment for passengers. On the other hand, External Elevator Button Sterilizers are attached to the exterior of the elevator doors, allowing passengers to sterilize the buttons themselves before entering the elevator. Both types aim to prevent the spread of germs and enhance the hygiene standards in public spaces.</p></p>
