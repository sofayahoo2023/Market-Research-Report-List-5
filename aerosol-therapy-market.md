<p><p>Aerosol therapy market consists of three main types of products: inhalers, nebulizers, and bronchodilators. Inhalers are handheld devices that deliver medication directly to the lungs through a metered dose of aerosol spray. Nebulizers are machines that convert liquid medication into a mist or aerosol that is inhaled through a mask or mouthpiece. Bronchodilators are medications that help relax and open up the airways in the lungs, making breathing easier for individuals with respiratory conditions like asthma or COPD.</p></p>
