<p><strong>The Copper Based Shape Memory Alloys Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Temperature Austenite Phase</li><li>Low Temperature Martensite Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper-based shape memory alloys are categorized into two main types based on their phase transitions: high temperature austenite phase and low temperature martensite phase. High temperature austenite phase alloys exhibit shape memory effect at elevated temperatures, making them suitable for applications in high temperature environments. On the other hand, low temperature martensite phase alloys show shape memory effect at lower temperatures, allowing for use in a wider range of applications including biomedical devices and actuators. These two types cater to different market needs based on their distinct phase transition temperatures.</p></p>
