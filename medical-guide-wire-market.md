<p><p>Medical guide wires are used in various medical procedures to help guide catheters and other medical devices through the body. There are three main types of guide wires: straight medical guide wires, angled medical guide wires, and J-shape medical guide wires. Straight guide wires are typically used for straightforward procedures, while angled guide wires are used for procedures that require a more precise path. J-shape guide wires are designed to navigate through specific anatomical structures. Each type of guide wire serves a different purpose in medical interventions.</p></p>
