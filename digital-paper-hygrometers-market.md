<p><strong>The Digital Paper Hygrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Digital Paper Hygrometers</li><li>Relative Digital Paper Hygrometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Paper Hygrometers can be categorized into two main types: Absolute and Relative. Absolute Digital Paper Hygrometers measure the exact amount of water vapor present in the air, irrespective of changes in temperature. On the other hand, Relative Digital Paper Hygrometers provide a percentage value of the water vapor present in the air in relation to the maximum amount of water vapor that the air can hold at a specific temperature. Both types of hygrometers are essential tools for monitoring and controlling humidity levels in various environments.</p></p>
