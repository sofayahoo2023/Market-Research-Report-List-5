<p><p>Clean Room Robot market includes various types such as Articulated Robots, SCARA Robots, Parallel Robots, Cartesian Robots, and Collaborative Robots. Articulated Robots have multiple joints allowing for a wide range of motion. SCARA Robots have a fixed base and can perform fast and precise movements. Parallel Robots have multiple arms connected to parallel joints for increased accuracy. Cartesian Robots move along a linear axis for precise positioning. Collaborative Robots can work alongside human operators in a shared workspace, ensuring safe and efficient operation in clean room environments.</p></p>
