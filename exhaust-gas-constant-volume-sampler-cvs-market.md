<p><ul><li>Manual (Single-Venturi System)</li><li>Automatic (Multi-Venturi System)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exhaust Gas Constant Volume Sampler (CVS) market offers both manual (Single-Venturi System) and automatic (Multi-Venturi System) types. The manual system requires operators to manually adjust the airflow using one venturi, whereas the automatic system utilizes multiple venturis for precise airflow control without manual intervention. The manual system is more cost-effective and suitable for smaller operations, while the automatic system provides more accurate and efficient sampling for larger-scale applications. Both systems play a crucial role in emissions testing and control in various industries.</p></p>
