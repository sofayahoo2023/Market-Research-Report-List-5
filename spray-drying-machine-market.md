<p><p>Spray drying machines are classified into three main types based on the method of atomization: Pressure spray dryer, stream spray dryer, and centrifuging spray dryer. Pressure spray dryer uses high-pressure nozzles to atomize the liquid into droplets. Stream spray dryer uses a rotary atomizer to produce fine droplets. Centrifuging spray dryer utilizes a centrifugal atomizer for atomization. Each type of spray drying machine has its unique advantages and applications in different industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
