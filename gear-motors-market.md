<p><p>Gear motors are widely categorized into three main types: Parallel Shaft Gear Motors, Right-Angle Shaft Gear Motors, and Planetary Gear Motors. Parallel Shaft Gear Motors are known for their high efficiency and compact design, making them suitable for various applications. Right-Angle Shaft Gear Motors are ideal for applications with limited space as they change the direction of power transmission at a 90-degree angle. Planetary Gear Motors offer high torque density and precision, making them suitable for industries requiring high precision and control.</p></p>
