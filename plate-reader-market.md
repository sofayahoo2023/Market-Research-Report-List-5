<p><p>Plate readers are an essential tool in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and academic research. Optical filter microplate readers utilize a specific wavelength of light to measure absorbance or fluorescence in samples, providing accurate and reliable data. On the other hand, optical grating microplate readers use diffraction gratings to split light into different wavelengths, allowing for more precise and versatile measurements. Both types of plate readers offer advanced technology and features to support a wide range of applications in research and development.</p></p>
