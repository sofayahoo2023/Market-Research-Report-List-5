<p><p>The Audible Emergency Warning System market offers two primary types of directional options: Omni Directional and Rotating Directional. Omni Directional systems emit sound in all directions simultaneously, providing widespread coverage in emergencies. On the other hand, Rotating Directional systems emit sound in focused directions, allowing for targeted alerts in specific areas. Both types serve different purposes in alerting individuals to emergencies, with Omni Directional being ideal for large open spaces and Rotating Directional for directing attention to specific locations.</p></p>
