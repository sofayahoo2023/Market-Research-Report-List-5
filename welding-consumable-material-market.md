<p><p>Welding Consumable Materials include Stick Electrodes, Solid Wires, Flux Cored Wires, SAW Wires, and Fluxes. Stick Electrodes are convenient for manual welding, Solid Wires are used for gas metal arc welding, Flux Cored Wires provide high deposition rates and are suitable for outdoor welding, SAW Wires are used for submerged arc welding, and Fluxes are used to protect the weld pool from atmospheric contamination. Each type of consumable material serves a specific purpose in the welding process, catering to different welding requirements.</p></p>
