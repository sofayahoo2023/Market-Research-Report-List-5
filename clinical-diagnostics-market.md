<p><ul><li>Instruments</li><li>Reagents</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clinical diagnostics market consists of three main types: instruments, reagents, and others. Instruments refer to the machines and equipment used for conducting diagnostic tests, such as X-ray machines, CT scanners, and DNA sequencers. Reagents are substances or chemicals used in laboratory analysis to detect, measure, or produce a reaction to a specific target, such as antibodies or enzymes. The "others" category includes consumables, accessories, software, and services related to clinical diagnostics that do not fall under instruments or reagents.</p></p>
