<p><p>PTCA Balloon Catheter Market Types are classified as Semi-Compliant, Non-Compliant, and Compliant. Semi-Compliant balloons have a moderate level of flexibility and are able to conform to the shape of the vessel during inflation. Non-Compliant balloons have minimal flexibility and exert a consistent force on the vessel walls, ideal for treating heavily calcified lesions. Compliant balloons have high flexibility and are able to conform to the vessel shape while maintaining a consistent pressure, suitable for dilating more delicate vessels.</p></p>
