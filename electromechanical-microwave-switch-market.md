<p><ul><li>Bypass Switch</li><li>Transfer Switch</li><li>Matrix Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electromechanical microwave switches come in three main types: bypass switch, transfer switch, and matrix switch. </p><p>Bypass switches allow for the direct connection of input to output without any disruption. Transfer switches provide seamless transition between two input ports to a single output port. Matrix switches, on the other hand, enable multiple input-output configurations for versatile signal routing.</p><p>Each type of switch caters to specific requirements in various applications, offering flexibility, reliability, and efficiency in managing microwave signals.</p></p>
