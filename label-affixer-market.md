<p><p>Label affixer market can be categorized into two types- semi-automatic and automatic. In semi-automatic label affixers, the operator needs to manually place the product on the machine for labeling, while in automatic label affixers, the process is fully automated with the machine handling the placement and affixing of labels. Semi-automatic label affixers are suitable for small-scale businesses with low production volumes, while automatic label affixers are more suitable for large-scale production facilities with high production volumes and require less manual intervention.</p></p>
