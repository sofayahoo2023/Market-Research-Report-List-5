<p><p>Anti-Parkinson Drugs Market Types include Dopaminergic drugs that directly replace dopamine, Dopamine Agonists that mimic the effects of dopamine, Enzyme Inhibitors that prevent the breakdown of dopamine, and other medications that help manage symptoms of Parkinson's disease. Dopaminergic drugs are the most common type used to treat Parkinson's disease by increasing dopamine levels in the brain. Dopamine agonists work by stimulating dopamine receptors, while enzyme inhibitors help maintain dopamine levels. Other medications target different aspects of the disease to provide relief for patients.</p></p>
