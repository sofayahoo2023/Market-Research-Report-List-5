<p><p>Multi-head weighing machines are used in various industries for precise weighing and packaging of products. There are two main types of multi-head weighing machines: linear and rotary. Linear machines are ideal for larger products and have a straight layout of weigh heads, while rotary machines are more suited for smaller products and have a circular arrangement of weigh heads. Both types offer high-speed weighing and accuracy, making them essential equipment for efficient packaging processes in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
