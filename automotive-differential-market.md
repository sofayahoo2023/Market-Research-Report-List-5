<p><p>Automotive differential market types can be categorized into all-wheel drive and two-wheel drive. All-wheel drive systems distribute power to all four wheels, providing better traction and stability in various driving conditions. This type of system is preferred for off-road driving and challenging terrains. On the other hand, two-wheel drive systems allocate power to either the front or rear wheels, offering a more straightforward and cost-effective solution for everyday driving on paved roads. Both options cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
